We create more than outstanding experiences.

Simplify your big data and analytics journey by choosing infrastructure that supports you no matter what your business outcomes are. Our portfolio provides batch or real-time needs focused on data lakes to self-service analytics.

Understanding your Big Data and Analytics use cases can be a challenge. Identifying the first step from a business and technology perspective can be overwhelming. Let us help you solve these challenges and get the most out of your Big Data environment.

Analytics requires having availability to all data. This is possible by removing silos and consolidating all your data into a Data Lake. Incorporating diverse sources and unifying data types by connecting your data gives you richer insights that will drive better business outcomes.

R & Python

Depending on your particular needs, our solutions can be created with R or Python. Our team is dedicated to giving our customers they services they need. If your company utilizes Jupyter notebooks we can develop solutions that allow you to keep and maintain them. Whether it be R or Python, we can work with your data. Allow our developers to speak with the members on your team to find out what works best for your business

Development

Depending on your company's budget, we can place 1 or more developers to work on your big data problems full-time. It's similar to having a consultant or contractor, except our employee can work directly with the members on our team to figure out the solution to any problem your company might face. We've worked with companies big and small and we know all the different issues that arise with companies of different sizes. Visit our contact page and let's get started on your problem!

Complexity

Businesses need complete technology solutions that reduce complexity. They want cloud functionality and solutions that are fully integrated and also integrated with on-premises platforms to deliver a seamless experience.

Focus on analytics, not infrastructure

We can help you incorporate GCP’s fully managed, serverless approach which removes operational overhead by handling your big data analytics solution’s performance, scalability, availability, security, and compliance needs automatically, so you can focus on analysis instead of managing servers. And thanks to innovative, customer-friendly pricing, you pay only for the resources you use — in some cases, by the second or the query. Flat-rate and sustained-use discounts are also available.

Research

Our goal at IceCold is to always utilize the latest research available to us in the field of Big Data. We make sure we use highly scalable solutions that best meet your needs. Whether that be something that can run on a single server or solutions that involve entire server farms. We make sure that the code we use utilize the newest instructions available on modern x86_64 processors so your business never misses a beat.
